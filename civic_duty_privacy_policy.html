Privacy Policy for iOS Civic Duty App

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

The developer of Civic Duty does not store, collect, use, or share any personal information on behalf of the users of Civic Duty (the application). All information is stored on the iOS device, and is never transmitted or uploaded.

Analytics

The only analytics that are collected are done so as part of Apple's iOS crash reporting, and there is no identifiable information that is collected along with these reports.

Conclusion

Quite simply, the app does not collect any personal information, because there's no reason to.
